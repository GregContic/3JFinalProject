Frequently Asked Questions

How many people are in each group?

Our groups typically have 6-10 members to ensure everyone gets adequate time to participate.

Do I need to commit to all sessions?

While consistent attendance is encouraged for the best results, we understand that life happens. Just let us know if you need to miss a session.

What if I'm nervous about sharing in a group?

You can participate at your own comfort level. There's no pressure to share until you're ready.

Can I try a session before committing?

Yes! We offer a free introductory session to help you determine if the group is right for you.
